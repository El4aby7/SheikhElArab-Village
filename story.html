Our Story

Established in the heart of the desert and brought to the city, Abu Khaled Village represents a journey of flavors and traditions passed down through generations.

Our founders believed that food is not just sustenance but a way to bring people together. Inspired by the Bedouin hospitality where every guest is treated like family, we strive to create an atmosphere of warmth and generosity.

From our slow-cooked Mandi prepared in traditional underground pits to our charcoal-grilled specialties, every dish tells a story of the ancient spice routes and the mastery of fire.
